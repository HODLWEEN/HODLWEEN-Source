FAQ

What is Hodlween?

HODLween is a bitcoin-themed Halloween party including live music, performances, open bar, hidden bitcoin, spectacular decor, and an epic costume contest for best Satoshi Nakamoto. It is the longest running Bitcoin Halloween party worldwide, brought to you by Bitcoin Charlotte.

HODLween is not a Web3, NFT, crypto, blockchain, or altcoin event. HODLween celebrates Bitcoin and bitcoiners ONLY!

Who is this party for?

This party is open to everyone. While it is hosted by bitcoiners, this is simply an extravagant bitcoin-themed Halloween party.

It's a celebration of those who have mastered the art of hodling, resisted the temptation to sell for short-term gains, and recognized that holding Bitcoin is the best way to preserve wealth, freedom, and self-sovereignty through generations.

HODLween also serves as a fun and social way to introduce Bitcoin to friends and family, making it an ideal gateway for newcomers.

Do I need to own or know about Bitcoin?

No! HODLween is designed for beginners and hodlers alike. If you are brand new to bitcoin, expect to learn a lot about the culture - we have plenty of fun memes for you to catch up on!

Is there a dress code / costume contest?

Costumes are STRONGLY encouraged! Get spooky, weird, sparkly, whatever your heart desires! Guests in costumes will have the chance to win bitcoin. The categories are “Best Satoshi Nakamoto", "Best couple/group", and "Best Overall". We can't wait to see what you come up with!

What is Bitcoin Charlotte?

Bitcoin Charlotte is a local Bitcoin community in Charlotte, NC founded in 2013. With 2500+ members and growing, Bitcoin Charlotte hosts in-person socials, networking events and workshops to educate the community about Bitcoin. Our mission is to spread knowledge to liberate individuals and businesses from financial dependence on banks and governments by leveraging the most superior money in history - Bitcoin.

What is TABConf?

TABConf is happening in Atlanta from Oct 23 - 26. TABConf’s mission is to create a forum for protocol and application developers to collaborate, debate, and innovate on Bitcoin. For more information and tickets CLICK HERE!

Where can I park?

We encourage uber, flying by broom, UFO’s, or rocket ships. However, if you do choose to drive there is on street parking and also space in the garage of Ambient+ Studio. Be safe!
