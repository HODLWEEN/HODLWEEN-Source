You don't need permission to be your own bank and you don't need permission to have fun!


HODLween is a Bitcoin only celebration designed to unite us around the idea of self sovereignty through play and community. If you hold bitcoin, are curious about bitcoin, or just want to have some fun - HODLween is for you!

Hodlween 2022 features three main events:
Fri, Oct 21st - Cattleman's Feast & Pumpkin Carving
Sat, Oct 22nd - Speakers & Bitcoin Workshops
Sat Night, Oct 22nd - HODLween Costume Party
