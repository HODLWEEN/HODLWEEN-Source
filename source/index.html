Follow Us

Follow us down the rabbit hole as we take you on a thrilling Halloween weekend adventure for the 3rd annual HODLween! HODLween features three main events, The Cattleman's Feast, HODLween Market, and a HODLween Costume Party. Satellite skeleton events will be held during the week and weekend.

Fri, October 20th

The HODLween adventure begins with the Cattleman's Feast! Hosted by The Beef Initiative, this event offers a farm-to-table meat-based dinner where attendees can indulge in delicious food sourced locally. Alongside the meal, there will be opportunities for socializing, enjoying music, and participating in a pumpkin carving competition. The Cattleman's Feast provides a family-friendly environment and a chance to connect with other Bitcoin enthusiasts.

Sat, October 21st

The HODLween Market offers a platform for various vendors to showcase and sell their products for bitcoin. Attendees can explore a wide variety of products, buy beer with bitcoin, listen to music, and hang with the local community. This will also be a chance for anyone to ask questions about bitcoin. This event is FREE for everyone!


The HODLween celebration reaches its peak with the Costume Party. Attendees are encouraged to dress up in their best costumes, with the suggestion of Satoshi Nakamoto-themed attire. The party promises live music, tarot readings, magic performances, and hidden bitcoin! It's a night of revelry, where participants can fully immerse themselves in the Halloween spirit.
